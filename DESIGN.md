# Design

## Direction
- Visual language: traditional East Asian mood, not generic wellness SaaS
- Tone: modern and data-forward, not mystical or vague
- Device priority: mobile first
- Supported locales: Korean, English, Chinese

## Visual Principles
- Use restrained, premium typography and spacing
- Use textures, patterns, or subtle symbolic motifs inspired by traditional East Asian culture
- Avoid loud superstition cliches
- Make paid value obvious before the paywall through a strong preview and clear category framing

## UX Principles
- The input flow must feel short even though birth data is sensitive
- Preview should create confidence, not frustration
- Payment should feel like a continuation of the report, not a hard context switch
- Saved results must be easy to scan by category and purchase type

## Accessibility
- Clear contrast and readable type at mobile sizes
- Avoid color-only signals for meaning
- Keep forms usable with keyboard and screen readers
