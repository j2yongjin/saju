# Product Sense

## Product Thesis
`Dongyangui Sinbiroun Unmyeong` is a paid fortune content app for users who want quick, polished, mobile-first saju insights. It combines structured saju calculation with AI-written interpretation across romance, compatibility, career, and wealth.

## User Priority
1. General users
2. Couple compatibility users
3. Professional fortune readers
4. Overseas Korean users

## Core Jobs To Be Done
- Help users understand themselves through saju-based interpretation
- Help users explore romance and compatibility questions
- Help users explore career and wealth themes

## Monetization
- One-time fixed-price purchases
- Bundle packaging for expanded reports
- Show a free one-paragraph preview before payment where useful

## Differentiators
- Traditional East Asian visual language
- Modern, data-forward interpretation tone
- Mobile-first experience
- Category-based reports and saved history

## Scope For MVP
- Social login
- Saju input
- Basic saju report
- Paid compatibility report
- Result history
- 1:1 inquiry board

## Explicit Non-Scope
- Medical advice
- Investment advice
- Legal advice
- Open-ended chat counseling
